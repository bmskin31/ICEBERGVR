Have you ever wondered what the world was like 30,000 years ago?   
Have you ever imagined great beasts, long-extinct, coming back to life?

Into the Ice aims to bring history roaring to life with our new virtual reality experience.
Using a combination of state-of-the-art technology and a fierce dedication to history, Into the Ice
transports you into the wild and vivid world of Palaeolithic Europe as never seen before.

THE VIRTUAL REALITY EXPERIENCE

With our patented state-of-the-art virtual reality technology, you are transported 30,000 years into the past for one whole hour of a highly immersive and realistic historical experience. Your first stop is The Valley, where you will join a band of reindeer hunters.


Next, you will push through a primeval forest to gather healing herbs to cure the sick.


Finally, you will accompany a shaman as you traverse a harsh mountain to reach a sacred cave.

As health and safety is our top priority, the VR experience will include breaks in order to give our guests a chance to rest their eyes.

VR HEALTH & SAFETY

Our virtual reality experience will require visitors to wear a VR headset and audio headset for up to 45 minutes (broken up by breaks) in order to get the optimum experience. The virtual experience is designed to immerse guests entirely in a different world, and though we are conscious of sensitivities that some guests may have, we are also dedicated to depicting the prehistoric world in an accurate, true-to-life way. This may include things that some guests may find upsetting or distressing:

  • Wild animals, including animals that may lunge or snap at the viewer.
  • Animal butchery, such as scenes of hunting.
  • Blood and gore as relating to the aforementioned scenes of hunting
  • Scenes of violence and fighting.
  • Yelling or shouting as relating to the characters featured in the VR experience.
  • High altitudes / heights from the viewer's point of view.
  • Sharp drops in perspective from the viewer's POV.
  • Underwater scenes.
  • Claustrophobic scenes, such as those set in caves or caverns.

THE ICEBERG© EDUCATION CENTRE

The famous Iceberg Education Centre precedes our VR experience, and was founded in 2019 to educate the public about prehistoric life, especially due to the large amount of new discoveries occurring around that time. It features a large museum, many artefacts both genuine and reconstruction, and interactive displays. It also houses our gift shop, where visitors can purchase mementoes to remember their experience, as well as a range of confectionery, books, toys, and crafts.
